About WeightLossCalc

Welcome to WeightLossCalc, your trusted partner in tracking weight loss progress. We believe that understanding your progress is the key to staying motivated on your health journey.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to provide accurate, easy-to-use tools that help people track their weight loss progress effectively. We understand that weight loss is a personal journey, and having the right tools can make all the difference.

Why We Created This Tool

Traditional weight tracking often focuses only on pounds or kilograms lost, but this doesn't tell the whole story. A 10-pound loss means something very different for someone who started at 150 pounds versus someone who started at 250 pounds.

That's why we created our Weight Loss Percentage Calculator - to give you a more meaningful way to measure your progress that accounts for your starting point.

Our Values

  • Accuracy: We provide precise calculations based on established formulas
  • Privacy: Your data never leaves your device - we don't store any personal information
  • Accessibility: Our tools are free to use with no registration required
  • Education: We provide resources to help you understand your progress better
