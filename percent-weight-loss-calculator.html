Percentage vs Pounds: Which is Better?

Both weight measurements have their place in tracking progress, but they serve different purposes:

Pounds/Kilograms

  • Easy to understand daily
  • Good for short-term motivation
  • Directly relates to clothing sizes
  • Simple to track with basic scales

Percentage

  • Better for long-term tracking
  • Accounts for your starting size
  • Medical standard for health assessment
  • Allows fair comparison between people

Our recommendation: Track both! Use pounds for daily motivation and percentage for assessing your overall progress and health improvements.
