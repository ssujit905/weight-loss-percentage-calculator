Frequently Asked Questions

How is weight loss percentage calculated?
Weight loss percentage is calculated using the formula: ((Starting Weight - Current Weight) / Starting Weight) Ã— 100. This gives you the percentage of body weight you've lost relative to your starting weight.
Why track weight loss percentage instead of pounds?
Percentage gives a better perspective of your progress relative to your starting point. Losing 10 pounds means different things for someone starting at 150 lbs vs 250 lbs. Percentage provides a standardized way to measure progress.
