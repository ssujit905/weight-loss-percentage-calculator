Which Countries Use Which System?

Understanding which measurement systems are used where can help you know when you might need to convert:

Kilograms (Metric System)

  • Most countries worldwide
  • All European countries
  • Australia, New Zealand
  • Canada (officially)
  • Most Asian countries

Pounds (Imperial System)

  • United States
  • United Kingdom (mixed use)
  • Canada (common usage)
  • Some Caribbean nations
  • Myanmar, Liberia

Note: Even in countries that officially use the metric system, pounds are often still used informally for body weight measurements.
