How to Calculate Your Weight Loss Percentage Easily

Tracking weight loss is much easier when you use **weight loss percentage** instead of just pounds or kilograms. It gives a clear picture of your progress regardless of your starting weight.

✅ Step-by-Step Weight Loss Percentage Formula

Formula:
(Starting Weight − Current Weight) ÷ Starting Weight × 100

Example:

If you started at 80 kg and now you weigh 74 kg:

(80 − 74) ÷ 80 × 100 = 7.5%
You have lost 7.5% of your weight.

Why Weight Loss Percentage Matters

✔ More accurate than tracking pounds alone

✔ Helps compare progress between people

✔ Shows long-term weight trends clearly
