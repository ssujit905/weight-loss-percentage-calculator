Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2025

Important: Our Weight Loss Percentage Calculator is designed with your privacy in mind. All calculations happen directly in your browser - we never see or store your personal weight data.

Information We Collect

We are committed to protecting your privacy. Here's what you need to know about the information we collect:

Personal Information

We do not collect any personal weight information. When you use our weight loss calculator:

  • Your weight data (starting weight, current weight) is processed entirely in your browser
  • We never transmit, store, or have access to your personal weight information
  • No registration or personal details are required to use our calculator

Automatically Collected Information

Like most websites, we collect some anonymous information through cookies and analytics:

  • Google Analytics: We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site (pages visited, time spent, etc.)
  • Cookies: We use cookies to improve your browsing experience and analyze site traffic
  • IP Address: Your IP address is collected anonymously for security and analytics purposes

How We Use Information

We use the anonymous information we collect to:

  • Improve our website and calculator functionality
  • Understand how visitors use our tools
  • Monitor and prevent any security issues
  • Provide aggregated statistics about website usage

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics: For understanding website traffic and user behavior
  • Google AdSense: To display relevant advertisements (if applicable)

These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect our website and your information. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure. The good news is that since we don't collect your personal weight data, there's no sensitive information to protect.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Use our weight loss calculator without providing any personal information
  • Disable cookies in your browser settings (though this may affect site functionality)
  • Contact us with any privacy concerns (see our contact page)

Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through our contact form.
