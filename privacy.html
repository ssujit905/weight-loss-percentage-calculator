Privacy Policy

How we protect your data and privacy when using our weight loss calculator

Last Updated: January 1, 2025

Important: Our Weight Loss Percentage Calculator is designed with your privacy as our top priority. All calculations happen directly in your browser - we never see, transmit, or store your personal weight data.

Information We Collect

We are committed to protecting your privacy. Here's what you need to know about the information we collect:

Personal Information

We do not collect any personal weight information. When you use our weight loss calculator:

  • Your weight data (starting weight, current weight) is processed entirely in your browser
  • We never transmit, store, or have access to your personal weight information
  • No registration or personal details are required to use our calculator
  • Your weight calculations remain private on your device

Automatically Collected Information

Like most websites, we collect some anonymous information through cookies and analytics:

  • Google Analytics: We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site (pages visited, time spent, etc.)
  • Cookies: We use cookies to improve your browsing experience and analyze site traffic
  • IP Address: Your IP address is collected anonymously for security and analytics purposes
  • Browser Information: We collect anonymous browser and device information for optimization

How We Use Information

We use the anonymous information we collect to:

Improve Our Tools

Enhance our website and calculator functionality based on user behavior patterns

Analyze Usage

Understand how visitors interact with our tools and content

Security Monitoring

Monitor and prevent any security issues or unauthorized access

Provide Statistics

Generate aggregated statistics about website usage and performance

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to enhance your experience:

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our website. This helps us improve our content and user experience. Google Analytics collects data such as:

  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Geographic location (country-level only)
  • Device and browser information
  • Referral sources

Google AdSense

We may display relevant advertisements through Google AdSense to support the maintenance of our free tools. These ads are contextual and don't use your personal weight data.

Important: These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them. We only work with reputable providers who comply with privacy regulations.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect our website and your information:

  • SSL encryption to secure data transmission
  • Regular security updates and monitoring
  • Limited data collection - we only collect what's necessary
  • Anonymous analytics - no personal identification in our data

The most important security feature is that your weight data never leaves your device. Since we don't collect your personal weight information, there's no sensitive data to protect on our servers.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Use our weight loss calculator without providing any personal information
  • Disable cookies in your browser settings (though this may affect site functionality)
  • Opt-out of Google Analytics tracking using browser extensions
  • Contact us with any privacy concerns (see our contact page)
  • Request information about what data we have collected (though it's all anonymous)

Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of any significant changes by:

  • Posting the new policy on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date
  • Adding a notice on our website for major changes
  • Where appropriate, notifying registered users (though our calculator requires no registration)

We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.

Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this privacy policy, please don't hesitate to contact us:

  • Through our contact form
  • For privacy-specific inquiries, mention "Privacy Policy" in your message

We typically respond to privacy-related inquiries within 24-48 hours.
